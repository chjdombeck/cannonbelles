Our Story

Rooted in
Cannon Falls

CannonBelles started as a simple love for good food and this community. What began with handcrafted cheese grew into something more — a full neighborhood destination offering coffee, ice cream, and the kind of food that reminds you where you are.

We're not trying to be trendy or corporate. We're just a local business that cares deeply about quality, our neighbors, and the idea that the best things in life are made by hand.

Every cup brewed, every wheel aged, every scoop served — all made with Cannon Falls in mind.
