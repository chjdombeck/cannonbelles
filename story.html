Where It All Began

Rooted in
Cannon Falls

CannonBelles didn't start as a business plan. It started as a passion. A love for real food, made the right way, shared with the people around you. That's still what drives everything we do.

What began as small-batch artisanal cheesemaking grew naturally into something more. The community responded, word spread, and CannonBelles became a full destination, adding a coffee and ice cream shop that's become a daily gathering place for locals and visitors alike.

We're proud to be part of Cannon Falls, a town that values real things, built by real people.

How We Got Here

The CannonBelles
Journey

The Beginning

A Passion for Craft

It started with a love of handcrafted cheese and a commitment to making something real, something the community of Cannon Falls could be proud of.

Growing Roots

The Community Responds

Word spread quickly. Neighbors, farmers markets, and loyal regulars made it clear: Cannon Falls was hungry for something local, honest, and made with care.

Expanding the Vision

Coffee & Ice Cream Opens

The same spirit that built the cheese business carried into a full coffee and ice cream shop, a warm, welcoming space for the whole community to gather.

Today

A Cannon Falls Destination

CannonBelles is now two businesses under one name, united by the same values that started it all: local, handcrafted, and made for the community.

"We're not trying to be trendy. We're just a local business that cares deeply about quality, our neighbors, and the idea that the best things in life are made by hand."
